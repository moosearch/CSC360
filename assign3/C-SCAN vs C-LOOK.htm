It seems a few students have completed their assignment 3 part 2 using C-LOOK, instead of C-SCAN. The difference is that C-LOOK changes the seeking direction as soon as there is no outstanding request in the same direction, while C-SCAN continues till the end of the disk.

For those students who have submitted their assignments, but not knowing what the difference between C-LOOK vs C-SCAN, I recommend to just leave it. The difference is not "huge", and I won't penalize your effort, as long as you explain it in your write-up (documentation).
